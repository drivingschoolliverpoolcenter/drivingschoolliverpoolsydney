Trust and safety

Parents and learners should know who they are contacting.

Before booking a first lesson, many families want to check that the instructor is approved, background checked and working in a properly insured dual-controlled vehicle. Those are sensible questions, and this page gives direct answers.

The Liverpool branch is presented with NSW accreditation signals, ADTA membership, National Police Check completion, Working with Children Check clearance and safer-driver program support. Those checks matter because the first lesson should feel safe, organised and clear from the beginning.

  • Government-approved driving instruction with NSW accreditation.
  • National Police Check and Working with Children Check completed.
  • Fully insured automatic vehicles with dual controls.
