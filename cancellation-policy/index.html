Policy overview

We understand that sometimes plans change.

Lessons are booked into reserved time slots, so cancellations and rescheduling requests need a clear policy. This helps protect both the student and the instructor’s time while still allowing reasonable flexibility when special circumstances arise.

If you need to cancel or move a lesson, the simplest approach is to contact the Liverpool branch as early as possible. The earlier we know, the easier it is to offer another lesson time and reduce the chance of a late cancellation fee.

  • Special circumstances can happen and are understood.
  • Lesson time is reserved specifically for the student.
  • Early communication makes rescheduling easier and fairer.

Notice period

Give at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule

The policy asks for at least 24 hours notice before the booked lesson time if you need to cancel or reschedule. This applies to ordinary lesson changes and gives the branch a fair chance to adjust the schedule.

Best option

Call or email as soon as you know the lesson needs to change.

Why the 24-hour window matters

It gives the instructor enough notice to reorganise reserved lesson time and reduces scheduling disruption.

Rescheduling is included

The same 24-hour guidance applies whether you are cancelling outright or moving the lesson to a different time.

Late changes and no-shows

Fees may apply when a lesson is cancelled too late or missed

A cancellation fee may apply if a lesson is cancelled within 24 hours of the appointment time. A fee may also apply in the case of a no-show. The reason is practical: the lesson slot has already been held aside and may not be able to be filled at short notice.

This page does not invent extra penalties or pressure language. It simply states the policy clearly so there is no confusion before booking. If you are unsure about a situation, contact the Liverpool branch directly and explain what has happened.

  • Late cancellation means within 24 hours of the booked lesson.
  • No-show means the student does not attend the reserved lesson without proper notice.
  • Contact the branch early if a genuine issue comes up.
