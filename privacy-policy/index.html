Policy overview

Committed to a clear, practical privacy standard.

Driving School Liverpool Centre is committed to providing a clear and reasonable privacy standard for students, parents and website visitors. This page is repurposed from the broader Sydney South privacy policy and rewritten so the visible branch context and contact details match the Liverpool operation.

The privacy policy sits alongside normal booking, contact and support functions. It explains what information may be collected, why that information is used, how it may be shared in limited circumstances and what options are available if someone wants access to their personal information.

  • Applies to enquiries, website use and lesson-related communication.
  • Uses Liverpool branch contact details, not the old multi-branch listing.
  • Keeps the wording direct and practical rather than overloaded with generic legal filler.

Collection of information

What information may be collected

Some parts of the website can be viewed without giving personal information. However, when a student or parent makes a booking enquiry, uses a form, requests support or shares lesson-related information, personally identifiable details may be collected.

Directly provided details

This may include contact information, booking details, messages, learner-related support details and other information sent through forms, email or phone-linked systems.

Site usage information

The website may collect non-personally identifiable information such as browser type, language, operating system, pages viewed, access times and referring website data.

Support-related information

If future support features are used, additional details may be provided for account recovery, service handling or communication purposes.

Use and sharing

Why the information is used and when limited sharing may happen

The general purpose of collecting information is practical. It helps manage enquiries, support bookings, respond to parents and learners, understand site usage patterns and improve the service experience over time. Non-personally identifiable visitor data may also be used internally for traffic analysis and content improvement.

Limited sharing may occur where another organisation or service provider is engaged to help with support, processing or related operational tasks. In those cases, only the information needed to perform that function should be used, and reasonable steps are expected to protect confidentiality and privacy obligations.

  • Used for bookings, enquiries, support and website improvement.
  • Non-personally identifiable browsing information may be collected for internal analysis.
  • Third-party access is limited to what is reasonably needed for service delivery.

Changes and updates

Privacy practices may be updated over time

If privacy practices change in the future, new uses or changes to the policy would apply from the updated version forward. Driving School Liverpool Centre also reserves the right to amend the policy when needed. If someone objects to the privacy policy, they should not continue using the site or related features.

Accessing your information

You can ask to access your personal information

A person may request access to their personal information, subject to the exceptions allowed by law. For security reasons, a written request may be required. A reasonable fee may also apply for searching for and providing access to that information on a per-request basis.
